TYPE OF FRUITS



Fruits can be eaten raw, frozen, stewed, cooked, or dried. All fruits may be classified into three major groups: simple, aggregate, or multiple.

S/No Type Details
1 Simple Fruits one fruit that has developed from the ovary of a single flower. Simple fruits may either be fleshy, like plums and peaches, or dry, such as walnuts and hazelnuts.
2 Aggregate Fruits a fruit formed from several ovaries of one flower that produces many tiny fruits clustered tightly together.
3 Multiple Fruits a fruit formed from the fusion of the ovaries of many different flowers which develop closely together to form one bigger fruit.
